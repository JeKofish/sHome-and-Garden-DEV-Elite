More information about our project

Idea

In the world where there is more and more modern buildings with gardens and greenhouses on the roof, we decided to enable owners and consumers easy maintainance and remote access. Our system contains a variety of sensors dependent of the needs an user wants.

Functionality

Our application is started by a web browser. You login to you profile. With the help of RaspberryPI, there is communication with all given sensors. You are enabled remote access in remote access you have rolling windows up and down, watering plants, purification of the air, measuring and maintaining temperatures.

We have 2D preview of greenhouse that can automaticaly water with the click on the label "Water" and it will work until you press "Stop"
